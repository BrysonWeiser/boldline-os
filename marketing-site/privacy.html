Privacy Policy

Last updated June 29, 2026

BoldLine Media ("BoldLine," "we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, and the choices you have. If you have questions, email us at theboldlinemedia@gmail.com.

Information we collect

  • Information you give us. When you fill out our contact form or the "find your fit" questionnaire, we collect what you submit — typically your name, business name, email address, and any message or details you choose to share. When you book a call, that scheduling is handled by Calendly and any details you enter there are governed by Calendly's own privacy policy.
  • Information collected automatically. Like most websites, our hosting provider records basic technical data (such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited) for security and reliability. We do not run third-party advertising or cross-site tracking on this website.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiry, answer questions, and follow up about working together.
  • To understand which package or approach might fit your business, based on what you tell us.
  • To operate, secure, and improve this website.

We do not sell your personal information, and we don't share it for others' advertising.

How we share information

We share information only with the service providers that help us run the site and respond to you, including:

  • Netlify — website hosting and form submission handling.
  • Supabase — secure storage of business data.
  • Calendly — call scheduling.
  • Email providers — to deliver and respond to messages.

These providers process information on our behalf, and we may also disclose information if required by law.

A note on advertising accounts

For clients, the Google and Meta advertising accounts we manage are owned and paid for directly by the client. We only hold manager-level access to run them; we never own or control a client's ad account or billing.

Cookies

This site uses only the minimal cookies needed to function. We do not currently use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. If we add website analytics in the future, we'll update this policy to say so.

Data retention

We keep the information you submit for as long as needed to respond to you and maintain our business records, then delete it when it's no longer needed.

Your choices

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information you've shared with us — just email theboldlinemedia@gmail.com and we'll take care of it.

Security

We use reputable providers and reasonable measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to keep your data safe.

Children's privacy

This website is intended for businesses and is not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll change the "last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your information? Email theboldlinemedia@gmail.com.

This policy is provided as a general, good-faith summary of our practices and is not legal advice. Consider having a professional review it for your specific situation.
