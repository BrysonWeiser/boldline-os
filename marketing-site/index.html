Questions

The things people ask first.

How much does this cost?
Management starts at $350/month, and every package above shows its starting price. Exact pricing depends on the platforms and tier that fit your goals, which we walk through on the call. On top of that, most clients run an ad budget of around $1,000/month or more, paid directly to Google or Meta. You always own your account.
Do I own my ad account?
Always. Your Google and Meta accounts stay in your name and are billed directly to you. We only ever hold manager-level access to run them, and we never hold, front, or touch your ad spend.
Why is there a three month minimum to start?
Because paid ads compound. The first month is mostly learning which keywords and audiences actually convert, the second puts that data to work, and by the third the system has real momentum you can judge fairly. Anything shorter sets both of us up to quit right before the results show. After those first three months we earn it month to month, and your budget never scales without your sign-off. We'll cover exact terms on the call.
How fast will I see results?
The first few weeks are mostly learning which keywords and audiences convert. Paid ads compound over time; they're not a light switch. And if your offer or follow-up isn't ready to make the most of the clicks, we'll tell you before you spend, not after. More on that here.
What if it's not working?
We review every account on a set schedule against real performance data and report in plain English, so you always know what's spending, what's converting, and what's wasted. If something isn't working, we change it. If we're genuinely not the right fit, we'll say so.
Which platforms do you run?
Google Ads and Meta, on their own or as one system, plus the landing pages, call tracking, and lead routing behind them. See the packages above.
