Questions

The things people ask first.

How much does this cost?
Our management fee depends on the platforms and tier you go with. We'll walk you through it on the call once we know what you need. On top of that, most clients run an ad budget of around $1,000/month or more, paid directly to Google or Meta (you always own that account).
Do I own my ad account?
Always. Your Google and Meta accounts stay in your name and are billed directly to you. We only ever hold manager-level access to run them, and we never hold, front, or touch your ad spend.
Am I locked into a long contract?
No long lock-ins. We earn it month to month, scaling your budget only once the numbers justify it and never without your sign-off. We'll cover exact terms on the call.
How fast will I see results?
The first few weeks are mostly learning which keywords and audiences convert. Paid ads compound over time; they're not a light switch. And if your offer or follow-up isn't ready to make the most of the clicks, we'll tell you before you spend, not after. (More on that here.)
What if it's not working?
We review every account on a set schedule against real performance data and report in plain English, so you always know what's spending, what's converting, and what's wasted. If something isn't working, we change it. If we're genuinely not the right fit, we'll say so.
Which platforms do you run?
Google Ads and Meta (Facebook and Instagram), on their own or as one system, plus the landing pages, call tracking, and lead routing behind them. See the packages above.
